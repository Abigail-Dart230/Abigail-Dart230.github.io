In The Rain

Today, I think I will dance in the rain
Despite the harsh freezing droplets falling onto my skin
I will dance until I can no longer

Today, I think I will sing in the rain
Despite the loud thunder in the distance overshadowing me
I will sing until my voice is gone

Today, I think I will love in the rain
Despite the warmth of the house calling our names
The warmth of your hand in mine is all I need

And it is all I will ever need

Fin.
